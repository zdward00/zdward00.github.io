Mind Map

A mind map is similar to a moodboard in that it serves as a space to collect related ideas. I made this mind map for an assignment in a digital art class. The assignment goal was to create an image that served as a visual representation of any subject. I chose my favorite season, fall.

Avocado Toast w/ Herbed Pesto

Here we see the illustrious avocado toast in all its glory. This particular spin was served at a coffee shop I worked at. This was the most ordered variation: sliced avocados, pesto spread, toasted hemp seeds, and preserved lemon.

Captive Birds

A photo captured during band practice with local band Captive Birds. The band has roots in Norfolk, Virginia and plays gigs locally and across states. They play a modern style of indie rock; think Death Cab for Cuties with slightly more edge. Pictured singing is band member Scott Rockswold, a childhood friend.

Synthesizer

Another photo taken during a Captive Birds band practice featuring Jake generating reverberating bass from their synth.

Flower Bloom

I took this photo at the first wedding I went to photograph. At the time, I wasn't an experienced wedding photographer, but a coworker was kind enough to let me tag along to get some experience. This was one of the few photos that turned out well.

Two Chairs

On a trip to Florida to attend my godfather's funeral with my family, I looked out of our window and saw these two chairs situated as they are in the picture. While I can only speculate that two people were chatting there before, I like to imagine it's a reminder of the closeness my dad and godfather shared.

Love Letters

Party favors from a wedding I worked at. When you plant the heart, it grows into a wildflower!

Relativity Booth

A photo from the Richmond Science Museum. To be quite honest, I don't remember what the Relativity Booth was. If memory serves correctly, this was in an exhibit highlighting renowned scientists and creatives.
